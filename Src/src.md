## Dictionary

### Numerical Variables:
  * Sale Price: The property's sale price in dollars. Predictions are based upon this variable.
  * Fireplaces: Number of fireplaces 
  * Year Built: Original construction date
  * Full Bath: Full Bathrooms above grade
